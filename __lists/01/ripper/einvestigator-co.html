Colorado Private Investigators and Investigation Agencies

Following are private investigators and private investigation agencies in the state of Colorado.  Before hiring a a detective agency in Colorado, contact the appropriate state licensing authority to confirm current licensing information. Colorado’s PI licensing program was just passed into law and licenses are issued by the Colorado Department of Regulatory Agencies, Office of Private Investigator Licensure. A business license may be required. You may also wish to contact the Professional Private Investigators Association of Colorado

Hire a Private Investigator in Colorado

The following list of private investigators and investigation agencies is in alphabetical order and is by no means a ranking. We encourage you to evaluate each detective’s services and select the most appropriate provider based on your individual needs. Take the time to review the private eye’s website (if available) and call several different P.I.’s to ask questions and get price quotes. To learn more about hiring a professional PI firm near you, and get more details on the costs involved, read our article Tips for hiring a P.I.
