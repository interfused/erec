Hire a Private Investigator in California

Following are private investigators and investigation agencies in the state of California. Before you hire a private investigator or agency in California, contact the appropriate state licensing authority to confirm current licensing information. To verify a private investigator license in the state, please visit the California Bureau of Security and Investigative Services website.

The following list of private investigators and investigation agencies is in alphabetical order and is by no means a ranking. We encourage you to evaluate each detective’s services and select the most appropriate provider based on your individual needs. Take the time to review the private eye’s website (if available) and call several different P.I.’s to ask questions and get price quotes. To learn more about hiring a professional PI firm near you, and get more details on the costs involved, read our article Tips for hiring a P.I.
