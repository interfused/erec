Hire a Private Investigator in Pennsylvania

If you need to hire a private investigator in Pennsylvania, please browse the following list of private investigators and agencies in the state. Before hiring a detective, contact the appropriate state licensing authority to confirm current licensing information. Visit the Pennsylvania State Private Investigator Association.

The following list of private eyes investigative agencies is in alphabetical order and is by no means a ranking. Before you hire a private investigator in Pennsylvania, we encourage you to evaluate each P.I.’s services and select the most appropriate provider based on your individual needs.  Take the time to review the detective’s website (if available) and call several different companies to ask questions and get price quotes.
