Hire a Private Investigator in Kentucky

If you need to hire a private investigator in Kentucky, browse this list of private investigators and investigation agencies in the state. Before making a hiring decision, contact the appropriate state licensing authority to confirm current license information. Visit the Kentucky Board of Licensure for Private Investigators. Also, visit the Kentucky Professional Investigators Association.
