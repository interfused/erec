South Dakota Private Investigators and Investigation Agencies for Hire

Following are private investigators and private investigation agencies in the state of South Dakota.  Before hiring a private investigator or private detective agency, contact the appropriate state licensing authority to confirm current licensing information.  In South Dakota, no Private Investigator license is required, but a business license through Department of Revenue is required. Visit the South Dakota State Revenue Office or call the Main Department of Revenue Office number at 800-829-9188.
